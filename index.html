Overview

ECE 6950 is a PhD level course that investigates the latest efficiency techniques used in deep learning. These are our learning objectives:

  • Get up to date with the latest research on deep learning efficiency.
  • Develop advanced understanding of existing efficiency methods for LLMs, SD, and NeRF.
  • Design and implement a deep learning efficiency project.
